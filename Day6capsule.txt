###cut command used by two ways:
 cut <options>
-c cut by characters
-f used to cut output by fields
-d define delimeter
cut c1 /etc/passwd to cut first characters of lines 
cut -c1-6 /etc/passwd to cut characters continuosly from first to sixth character
cut -c1,3 /etc/passwd to get first or third character only
cut -d ":" -f1 to used to cut first field only
awk command comes in seen when cut command fails at certain amount of delimeter when space is given.
awk calculates spaces automatically
